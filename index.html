Noodles

  • Time: 8mins
  • Serves: No servings.
Ingredients Quantity
Ramen Noodles 2 packs
Spices(pepper and salt) 1 kg per item
Sausages 1 Roll

Step 1: Boil water in a pot for 2 minutes.
Step 2: Put the Ramen Noodles in the boiling water to cook. Cook for 4 minutes.
Step 3: Add the Spices, stir it with the noodles.
Step 4: Add the Sausage with the noodles and serve!!! .

Red Soup

  • Time: 32mins
  • Serves:2 servings
Ingredients Quantity
Tomatoes 28 ounces of Tomatoes
Onion A bushel of onions
Oil Vegetable oil.
Spices Salt, pepper and spice cubes

Step 1: Put the Vegetable Oil in a frying-pan and heat for 2 mins.
Step 2: Dice the Onions and pour it into the hot oil. Let it fry for 5 mins.
Step 3: Pour the Tomatoes Cover the pan with a lid and leave for 10mins on the fire.
Step 4: Add the Spices; a pinch of salt, 1/2 of the pepper and a cube of "spice cubes"
Step 5: Stir and leave for 20mins.
Step 6: Switch off the heat and serve with rice.
